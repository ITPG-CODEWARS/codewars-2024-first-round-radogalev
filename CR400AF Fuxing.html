CR400AF Fuxing

Max Speed
350km/h

Engine Power
17 701HP

The CR400AF Fuxing train is a marvel of modern engineering, representing China’s cutting-edge advancements in high-speed rail technology. Part of China Railway’s Fuxing (“Rejuvenation”) series, this train model is one of the fastest and most technologically sophisticated in the world. With a top operational speed of 350 km/h (about 217 mph), it can cut travel time significantly, making it the premier choice for business and leisure travel alike. Here's a closer look at what makes the CR400AF Fuxing train stand out:
Key Specifications and Features
Top Speed: The CR400AF Fuxing can reach speeds of up to 350 km/h (217 mph) in regular operation, though it is designed to hit a maximum speed of 400 km/h (248 mph) in ideal conditions.
Aerodynamic Design: The CR400AF model features a sleek, streamlined nose that reduces air resistance, enhancing speed and stability even at high velocities. This design also reduces energy consumption, making it an eco-friendlier choice.
Capacity and Layout: This model comes in an 8-car formation with a seating capacity of around 556 passengers. Seats are designed for comfort and come in First Class, Second Class, and Business Class options, catering to a range of passenger preferences.
Quiet and Comfortable Ride: Engineered with advanced noise reduction technology, the CR400AF ensures a quieter cabin environment, even at top speeds. Passengers can enjoy the scenery or get work done without distraction.
Safety Features: Safety is paramount in the Fuxing series. The CR400AF has an advanced monitoring system that continually checks for any irregularities and can automatically adjust speed or stop in case of emergencies. In extreme conditions, such as earthquakes, the train is equipped to stop automatically, ensuring passenger safety.
Real-Time Information Systems: Each cabin is equipped with digital displays that provide passengers with real-time updates on travel speed, arrival times, and other key information. The information systems are designed to keep passengers informed throughout their journey.
