Here you can see all of the trains that are included in the TracksPassion catalog.

Shinkansen

Japan’s high-speed “bullet train” known for its punctuality, comfort, and speeds up to 320 km/h, connecting major cities with cutting-edge technology.

TGV

France’s high-speed train, the TGV (Train à Grande Vitesse), reaches speeds of 320 km/h, offering fast, efficient travel across France and into neighboring countries.

ICE

Germany’s Intercity-Express (ICE) is a high-speed train renowned for its smooth ride and modern amenities, traveling up to 300 km/h across Germany and Europe.

CR400AF Fuxing

China’s ultra-modern high-speed train, reaching up to 350 km/h, connecting major cities quickly and offering advanced comfort features.

Eurostar

The high-speed train that connects London with Paris, Brussels, and Amsterdam through the Channel Tunnel, known for efficient cross-border travel.

Acela Express

The fastest train in the United States, Acela connects Boston, New York, and Washington, D.C., reaching speeds up to 240 km/h.

Freicciarossa 1000

Italy’s premier high-speed train, reaching speeds of 300 km/h, known for luxury and speed, connecting cities across Italy with elegance.
